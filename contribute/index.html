Contribute

You can help in taking care of the expenses involved in getting any particular child educated up to a certain level or you can also be a joint sponsor of such an endeavour. Alternatively you can contribute/donate a specific sum to the trust which will go into the general pool of money and used for helping a number of children, the number depending upon the total resources available and the actual requirement of funds.

We follow the policy of not charging any expense to the trust so that the entire donation goes towards the intended purpose without any diversion.

It is our policy not to reveal the real identity of the children in need, on the website or any such media publication. However donors are welcome to get involved with the family in need, on a personal basis and interact directly with the family.

We have detailed a list of methods in which our donors can participate:

  1. Through donations in cash or cheque to help in the education of needy students (Donations above Rs. 500/- are accepted)
  2. Through donations in kind like Text books, dresses and stationary items
  3. By supporting or sponsoring a child’s education every year.
  4. By sponsoring students for training, work shops, seminars etc.
  5. By helping students bag projects/internships which may be required as part of their curriculum
  6. By helping them in Job Placements
  7. You can remit your donations to the foundation’s account
