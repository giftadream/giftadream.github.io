Objective

The objective of this trust is to identify children who have the desire to pursue their education but are unable to do so, on account of financial constraints.
The selection procedure involves a thorough check on the financial background of their family as well their intent in educating their children further. The extent of help required is then decided. There is no bias in terms of religion and other extraneous considerations except for a positive bias towards the Girl child. Once a case is taken up we make all efforts to see that their education up to a certain level is taken care of so that they can take up suitable jobs and start earning a reasonable livelihood. Efforts will be made to see that these children get good placements also.

  1. To assist the needy and deserving students irrespective of caste, creed and gender to help them in their studies
  2. By providing Text books, Note books, daily wear dresses school/college bags, Stationery items.
  3. By paying their fees, by helping them access to libraries, sending them to participate in workshops/trainings, by sending them to job oriented short term courses, support them in their Project work.
  4. To promote activities related to education.
  5. To undertake activities resulting in relief and alleviation of poverty and distress. To help the public at large irrespective of caste, creed gender and nationality during natural calamities.
  6. To aid needy persons for medical treatment surgical operations etc

MISSION

To help children from economically challenged classes gain access to equal opportunities in the field of education and enable them to be self reliant.
