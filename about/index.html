About

MISSION

To help children from economically challenged classes gain access to equal opportunities in the field of education and enable them to be self reliant.

TRUST REGISTRATION

The Trust was registered on 17th August 2010. The total cost of which was around Rupees Ten Thousand. This expenditure was borne by the trustees.

The Donations to the Trust is now exempt under 80G of Income Tax Act.

 

PROFILE OF TRUSTEES

A trustee has been involved for more than 30 years in the field of Education, initially as a teacher and later on as the Principal of a reputed institute in Bangalore. She has firsthand knowledge of not only the academic field but also has realised the importance of education in moulding the career of children of this country. The fact that many children otherwise capable were unable to pursue their education beyond the Higher Secondary level only because of their financial constraints made her decide to pitch in financially to help such children through a small informal group of likeminded persons. She has also actively involved with a orphanage and an old age home in Bangalore.

A trustee retired as a Manager in Canara Bank for more than 30 years and has worked all over India. He took voluntary retirement in 2001 and since then involved himself in various social and cultural organisations

A trustee an Electrical Engineering graduate by qualification, Fellow of Institution of Engineers (India), Member, Institute of Industrial designers (U K), retired as Deputy Director of Union Ministry of Industry. He also served in the United Nations as Industrial development Executive in Libya for 20 years and is now settled in Bangalore.

A trustee is an Engineer working in Software profession (Tata Consultancy Services) and hails from Sivaganga in Tamil Nadu. Though young he is involved actively in many social activities and has helped many persons in pursuing their education, provided medical treatment for many needy persons. He is right now in Singapore for a year on a project.

A trustee is an Electronics and Communications Engineer and is working with Robert Bosch Engineering and Business Solution in Bangalore. He has already helped many persons in pursuing their education and is now on a Sabbatical and pursuing higher studies in Engineering in Germany.

A trustee is a Commerce graduate with a diploma in insurance. He is a member of the chairman club for agents having serviced many families and 2500 insurance policies. He is also a member of the Globally recognized MDRT, headquartered in Illinois USA. He believes that knowledge is power and strives for class, consistency and commitment through sustained hard work and perseverance. Above all he is a young man with a heart of gold, always helping the needy in their education. He has been involved in philanthropic activities since the time he graduated. His presence in the trust as a member has brought in lot of positive results.

A trustee is a manager with IBM and hails from Pune. He is an IIT, IIMA alumnus. He is of the firm belief that education alone can help society grow. Equal opportunity for all is his dream. By joining Gift a Dream Foundation, he has found a priceless way to give back to society what society has given to him.

A trustee is a B E from Motilal Nehru Regional College of Engineering [M N R C E] in Allahabad and M B A from Indian Institute of Management [I I M] in Ahmadabad. Our Trust is her brainchild. She has been supporting needy students even before the Trust was formed. She is also instrumental in getting good number of donations in to the Trust.
