Welcome

“Man was born free, and he is everywhere in chains”

- Jean Jacques Rousseau (Opening line of Social Contract published in 1762)

This statement has stood the test of time for over two centuries now. A cursory understanding of Modern society today reveals these deep inequalities in every sphere of life, from the basic necessities of life like education , health-care, housing, gainful employment and other peripheral functions of the society like culture, entertainment and sports. This entrenched inequality stems mainly from the economic background of men. And the goal of our foundation is to take a small step to bridge this inequality in the area of opportunity and access to quality education.

MISSION

To help children from economically challenged classes gain access to equal opportunities in the field of education and enable them to be self reliant.
